About

Hi, I'm Aryan Medam — a filmmaker and composer passionate about the intersection of image and sound. My work explores how music shapes cinematic experience, and how visuals can carry emotional weight beyond words.

I write, direct, and score my own short films, drawing influence from composers like Ennio Morricone, Jonny Greenwood, and Ryuichi Sakamoto, and filmmakers like Andrei Tarkovsky and Wong Kar-wai.

When I'm not on set or at the piano, I'm building tools that bring music and technology together — including the metronome and tuner on this site.
